About Fazbear Entertainment

1983

Fredbear's Family Diner opens its doors, featuring the first animatronic characters: Fredbear and Spring Bonnie.

1987

Rebranded as Freddy Fazbear's Pizza with new characters: Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy.

1990

The "Freddy and Friends" TV show debuts, making our characters household names.

1993

First franchise location opens. Over 50 locations nationwide by 1995.

Our Mission

At Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, we believe in creating magical experiences for children and families through delicious food, exciting games, and unforgettable entertainment with our world-famous animatronic band.
